This Privacy Policy describes how Item Remindly ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Information

Our App requires access to your device's location to provide its core functionality:

  • Current Location: To determine when you're leaving saved locations
  • Saved Locations: Locations you save for item reminders (home, work, etc.)
  • Background Location: To send reminders when you're not actively using the App

1.2 App Usage Information

  • Items you add to your reminder lists
  • Categories you create and use
  • Notification preferences and settings
  • App performance and crash data (for improving the App)

1.3 Subscription Information

  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Payment information (processed securely by Apple/Google)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide location-based reminder services
  • Send notifications when you're leaving saved locations
  • Manage your subscription and premium features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety
  • With service providers who assist in app operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Most of your data is stored locally on your device:

  • Item lists and categories
  • Saved locations
  • App preferences and settings

4.2 Cloud Storage

Some data may be stored in secure cloud services for:

  • Subscription management
  • App analytics and crash reporting
  • Backup and synchronization (if enabled)

4.3 Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication methods
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your data and account
  • Control location permissions through device settings
  • Opt out of certain data collection
  • Request data portability

6. Location Services

Important: Our App requires location access to function properly. You can control location permissions through your device settings, but limiting location access will reduce the App's functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the App and updating the effective date.
